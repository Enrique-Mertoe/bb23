Consultancy Services

Overview

This brochure provides an in-depth look at the consultancy services offered by Bob Grogan Consulting Ltd. It highlights our expertise in strategic planning, organizational development, regulatory compliance, and business transformation.

Is your organization a candidate for consultancy services?

Organizations are faced with complex challenges that require use of expert knowledge and tools to analyze, and with evidence-based insights, develop solutions and frameworks to address challenges using structured and tested approaches and models. Like a doctor in medical practice, our role is to listen to clients, carry out organizational diagnosis and facilitate informed discussions aimed at crafting solutions and strategies that work.

Our Consultancy Offerings

Crafting effective strategies that align with organizational goals. Our experts undertake situation analysis through desk reviews and interviews with stakeholders and facilitate strategy workshops where vision, mission, core values, strategic goals, and implementation frameworks are crafted through a collaborative and participatory approach.
Enhancing organizational structures, processes, and culture for improved performance. We generate evidence through surveys and gaps analysis to inform review of systems, structures, plans, and processes.
Navigating the complexities of regulatory frameworks and ensuring compliance. We conduct mock audits and gaps analysis to establish the status of compliance with standards and regulatory frameworks upon which we design training and other relevant interventions to close the gaps. Our mainstay is ISO management systems.
Guiding businesses through change initiatives to enhance efficiency and competitiveness. We work with organizations to craft their ICT strategies, automate their processes, and migrate them to a 100% paperless roadmap.

Our Consultancy Process

We undertake initial sensitization sessions where we establish rapport and work with our clients to develop roadmaps pointing to areas of engagement between us and our clients. We further are able to share consultation scheduling and project scoping. During the sensitization sessions we share success stories of our past clients
